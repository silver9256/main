Motherboard

This computer part is where you connect all the basic components like the processor, video card, storage, power supply,etc. One important thing that we need to remember is that motherboard differs depending on what processor you will use. There are two popular motherboards. These are the intel and amd motherboard.

We can see its physical differences when it comes to processor socket. Intel socket has pins inside the socket while amd only have holes for the pins of processor. We should always be aware of this things because if you try to insert a intel processor into a AMD motherboard your bought computer parts will be break which also means that you waste your money. So, it is practical to be sure when we are buying computer. You can search the google for the right parts for your computer build.
