Graphics Card

Graphics Card are responsible for the visuals of our computer. It helps the computer to display higher quality of information. Some processors have its own graphics processor to display and it is called integrated graphics while other processors needs a dedicated graphics processor.

One of the reason for you to buy a graphics is that your processor doesnt have integrated graphics or you want to upgrade your graphics experience. If you plan to buy one, there are two popular brands of graphics card and it is Nvidia and AMD.
