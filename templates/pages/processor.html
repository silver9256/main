Central Processing Unit

The processor is the brain of the computer. It performs calculation, input/output and the basic instructions that other components of the computer needs.

There are two popular processors for computer. These are the processors made by Intel and AMD.

AMD processor are mostly used for gaming for its numerous core inside the processor.

While Intel processor are commonly used in workstation because of its efficiency and compatibility when it comes to production software.
